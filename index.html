About MobilErgon

MobilErgon is a new software company, based in Greece, that is focused on the design and development of mobile applications.

Currently, we have expertise in designing and developing applications for the two of the major mobile platforms and OSes:

  • Nokia Symbian OS (MeeGo OS soon)
  • Google Android OS

Very soon, MobilErgon will expand its support to the other known mobile platform/OS, Apple iOS (iPhone, iPod Touch, iPad).
